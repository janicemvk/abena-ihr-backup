QUANTUM CIRCUIT DESCRIPTIONHealthcare Optimization Model

4-Qubit Integrative Treatment Optimization Circuit
Each qubit represents a critical treatment factor:
Qubit 0: Conventional pharmaceutical effectiveness
Qubit 1: Herbal medicine compatibility
Qubit 2: Lifestyle intervention impact
Qubit 3: Patient genetic factors
Circuit Architecture
Initialization Layer: Hadamard gates on all 4 qubits, creating equal superposition (1/√16 amplitude across all 16 treatment combinations) for quantum parallel exploration of the entire treatment space.
Entanglement Layer: CNOT gates model interactions: CNOT(0,1) for drug-herb; CNOT(1,2) for herb-lifestyle; CNOT(2,3) for lifestyle-genetics. This creates quantum correlations representing real-world treatment dependencies.
Optimization Layer: Parameterized rotations encode treatment optimization objectives: RY(π/4) on qubit 0 (Drug dosage), RY(π/3) on qubit 1 (Herbal formula), RY(π/6) on qubit 2 (Lifestyle intensity), and RY(π/4) on qubit 3 (Genetic factor weighting).
Measurement Layer: Full measurement on the computational basis collapses the superposition to a classical treatment recommendation, with the probability distribution reflecting optimal treatment combinations.
